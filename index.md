# Privacy Policy for Patterna

Last updated: December 2025

Patterna respects your privacy. This application is designed to work fully offline and does **not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data**.

## Information Collection and Use
Patterna does **not collect any personal information**.
All data entered into the app (such as symptoms or notes) is stored **locally on your device only**.

## Data Storage
- All data remains on the user's device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No cloud storage is used

## Third-Party Services
Patterna does **not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services**.

## Children's Privacy
Patterna does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy
If changes are made to this privacy policy, the updated version will be published on this page.
